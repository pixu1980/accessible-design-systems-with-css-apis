What is a Design System?

A Design System is a shared
language used to define and
collect a set of guidelines

Why do we need an
Accessible Design System?

Key Benefits

  • Consistency
    Coherent look and feel across all products
  • Scalability
    Adaptive components as the product grows
  • Efficiency
    Streamlines design and dev workflows
  • Accessibility
    Inclusive design practices at its core

Strategic Impacts

  • Empowers brand identity
  • Reduces design and technical debts
  • Accelerates time-to-market
  • Drives quality and innovation

Systemic Accessibility

  • Color contrasts and guidelines for visibility
  • Spacing and grid systems for clear structure
  • Typography and iconography for legibility
  • Visual modes and responsive design guidelines
  • Motion guidelines, with reduce motion fallbacks
